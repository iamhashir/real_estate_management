# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Real estate agents and brokers who manage property listings, client pipelines, and deal flows. Used primarily at a desk or laptop during working hours — reviewing dashboards, tracking commissions, monitoring active listings.

## Product Purpose

A premium real estate CRM and listing management dashboard. Helps agents stay on top of their pipeline, recent listings, and performance metrics in one place. Success means agents spend less time in spreadsheets and more time closing deals.

## Brand Personality

Refined, authoritative, confident. The feel of a high-end architecture firm's internal tool — precise, luminous, unhurried. Not a startup SaaS; more like a private-banking dashboard.

## Anti-references

- Generic SaaS dashboards (HubSpot, Salesforce) — corporate gray, no personality
- Dark-mode "hacker" tools — this is a bright, daylight workspace
- Bubbly consumer apps — too playful for a professional deal-flow tool

## Design Principles

1. **Daylight precision** — the interface lives in bright, open light; glass catches the light rather than absorbing it
2. **Restraint over decoration** — every visual element earns its place; no decorative chrome
3. **Data at a glance** — hierarchy and contrast do the heavy lifting so agents read the room instantly
4. **Quiet confidence** — premium without shouting; luxury through craft, not ornament

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. Support reduced motion via `prefers-reduced-motion`. All text at ≥4.5:1 contrast against their background.
